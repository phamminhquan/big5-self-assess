Big 5 Personality Self Assessment

About: This is a webapp intended for self-assessment of the "Big 5" personaility traits. The "Big 5" theory states that one's personality can be decomposed into 5 main dimensions:

  • Extraversion: motivated by social interaction, gain energy from and enjoy engaging with people
  • Neuroticism: strong reaction to negative emotions, low tolerance to stress
  • Conscientiousness: self-disciplined, dutiful, stubborn, focused
  • Openness: greatly appriciate variety of new experiences, willing to try new things
  • Aggreableness: willingness to compromise self-interests for others
Note that these personality traits are not moral virtues, meaning there is no "good" and "bad" point on any of the 5 personality dimensions. More details about how the "Big 5" was constructed will be added in the Appendix section at the end of the page. Below is a self-assessment survey that reveals where you are (percentile wise) on the 5 personality dimensions. Details about the data and statistical method used for this self-assessment test will be added in the Appendix section.
